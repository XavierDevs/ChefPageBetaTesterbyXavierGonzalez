    Welcome

    Always happy to see you

    We believe that good experiences create unforgettable moments, that is why we take care of every step in the
    process and we strive to provide you with the best quality service. Thank you for choosing us.

    Our team of passionate and talented chefs are ready to transform any occasion into an unforgettable culinary experience. We specialize in using fresh, quality ingredients. Plus, we take care of all the logistics, allowing you to relax and enjoy while we take care of the preparation, serving and cleaning.

    Chef

    Edwin Viveros

    Originally from Tulum, Q.roo, from a very young age he was passionate about local and traditional Mayan gastronomy, he led a small restaurant in the coastal area of ​​Tulum, being responsible for the creation of the menu and kitchen supervisor, with 6 years of experience. With experience and a lot of dedication, it was decided to create this project with Alfredo and thus share experiences and unique dishes from the region.

    Chef

    Alfredo Pech

    Born in Yucatan, he has always been interested in traditional Mayan gastronomy, fusing the art of haute cuisine, his extensive knowledge of local and unique ingredients from the region creating unique experiences for each diner. With more than 20 years behind him, he has worked in luxury resorts and restaurants in the coastal area, being in charge and awarded for his leadership and performance.
